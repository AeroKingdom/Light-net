PROTECTING YOUR CHILDREN IS OUR PRIORITY

The SAFE Campus is designed to increase awareness of significant threats to schools in order to predict them and intervene prior to escalation.

The SAFE Campus was developed by Blue Light in response to the number one threat to our schools today, violence. After becoming involved in the assessment of the Parkland School Shooting in Fort Lauderdale, Blue Light recognizing that its over 15 years in predictive analytics and data integration could be applied to better predicting school violence and The Safe Campus was born.

The SAFE Campus is a platform of proven, cutting edge, out of the box technologies that, when combined, provide schools with a powerful picture of threats against students, parents, facilities and educational staff and the ability to remain vigilant against new threats as they emerge.

The SAFE Campus is designed to be affordable for schools of all sizes by leveraging existing school and local government IT infrastructure and data sources, minimizing customization yet providing a solution that is focused solely on that school district.
